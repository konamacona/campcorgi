Our Resort

Corgi Camp is the premier destination for high status corgis. We provide 24 hour service to meet all a Corgi's needs. Our Walk, Train, Play program is revolutionary and world renown for being enjoyed by Corgi's everywhere.

Exercise is an important part of anyone's routine, that's why we take our guests on multiple walks everyday, including a 1.5 km walk, which may include running! Our camp includes advanced training classes every day to help our guests be their best selves. Finally, nightly play sessions make sure they feel loved and don't get bored.
